Annapurna College Of Film And Media, Hyderabad


Annapurna College of Film and Media was established in 2011 in the heart of Hyderabad. The institute is a full-time member of CILECT. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses, namely, B.A. and M.A. in a variety of film, media, and liberal arts subjects. There are also weekday and weekend certificate courses, as well as dual specialization courses that cover various aspects of film-making. Candidates can apply for these courses by filling the online application. Admissions are based on marks scored in the qualifying exam, entrance exam, group discussion, and interview. The college has Placement Partners like Annapurna Studios, Arka Media Works, Asandwhen, Boxcar Caravan, Digital Domain, First Show, Guru Films, India Ahead to provide students with ample opportunities.

Anupam Kher's Actor Prepares - The School For Actors, Mumbai


Among India's finest acting school is Anupam Kher’s Actor Prepares – The School of Actor. The school was established in the year 2005 by Anupam Kher It is the one-stop solution for those who aspire to make a mark in the film industry. The college is known to offer the aspirants with a professional level course involving specialized training along with theoretical knowledge. The curriculum is set in a constructive manner that involves faculty dealing with students in the way of games, discussions, exercises and so on. The school has professional-level courses which provide specialized training in acting and screenwriting. Apart from this, there is also a roster of Bollywood celebrities as school’s visiting faculty.

Whistling Woods International, Mumbai


Whistling Woods International (WWI), Mumbai is Asia’s premier Film, Communication and Creative Arts Institute. Founded by one of India’s leading filmmakers – Subhash Ghai, WWI is one of the largest and best media institutes in the country. ‘The Hollywood Reporter’ has rated Whistling Woods International as one of the Ten Best Film Schools in the world. The institute was also awarded Excellence in Creative & Performing Arts at the 6th FICCI Higher Education Excellence Awards 2019. WWI offers programs that vary in duration from 1 year to 4 years catering to all major verticals of the Media & Entertainment industry, namely Acting, Cinematography, Direction, Editing, Producing, Production Design, Screenwriting, Sound Recording & Design, Visual Effects, Animation & Game Design, Media Management, Fashion Design, Music Production & Composition, Visual Communication Design and Event Management. All the programs are accredited by Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development and the Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS). Admission to WWI Mumbai is done on the basis of the General Aptitude Test, Creative Ability Test, Personal Statement of Purpose and Personal Interview. Additionally, WWI has provided 90% placement to its students in the previous year. More than 2500 alumni are successfully working in the Media & Entertainment industry. The average package is recorded at INR 4 LPA and the highest package is recorded at IBR 8 LPA. Some of the top employers are Adfactors, Prime Focus, Byjus, Star Network, Lakeshore, etc.

Film and Television Institute of India, Pune


Film and Television Institute of India, Pune was established in 1960 and is one of the premier colleges of the country. Over the years, the Institute has given several leading personalities to both the film and television industries. According to CEOWORLD Magazine, it is one of the top 10 film schools in the world. The Institute offers several Certificate and Short term courses under the Film and TV wings. The selection of the candidates is based on Joint Entrance Test (FTII JET), Interview and Orientation. In 2019, the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) recognized five courses offered by FTII – Direction; Electronic Cinematography; Video Editing; Sound Recording and TV Engineering; and Feature Film Screenplay Writing. Read more: FTII Admissions 2022 The Institute offers various facilities including accommodation, gym, playground for sports events and Film Libraries. Scholarships are also provided to the worthy students. FTII is an active member of the Centre International de Liaison des Ecoles de Cinéma et de Télévision (CILECT). Additionally, every year, it has collaborated with several TV, film and media schools around the globe in order to conduct student exchange programmes.
